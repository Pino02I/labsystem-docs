LABsystem Documentation
=======================

Welcome to LABsystem - Professional Camera & Render Ecosystem for Blender.

LABsystem is a modular addon that streamlines your workflow with camera management, 
world/HDRI control, rendering operations, lighting control, and timeline tools.

.. note::
   **Quick Start**: Install the addon, press N in 3D View, find the LABsystem tab.

What's Inside
-------------

**CameraLAB** - Create, switch, and manage cameras with resolution presets

**LightLAB** - Control all lights from one place with batch operations

**WorldLAB** - Assign HDRIs per-camera with auto-sync and rotation control

**FrameLAB** - Quick frame range presets for your timeline

**RenderLAB** - Smart rendering with viewport visibility and automatic backups
