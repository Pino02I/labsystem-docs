# Interface

LABsystem can be accessed in three different ways. Use whichever fits your workflow.

## Sidebar Panel (Main Interface)

Press **N** in the 3D View to open the sidebar, then click the **LABsystem** tab.

Each enabled module appears as a separate panel:
- CameraLAB
- RenderLAB
- LightLAB
- FrameLAB

This is where you'll find all controls and settings.

## LABwindow (Quick Access)

Press **F1** (default) to open a floating window with all LABsystem tools.

**When to use:**
- Quick operations without opening the sidebar
- When you need temporary access
- Working with limited screen space

**Close:** Press F1 again or click outside the window

## LABmenu (Marking Menu)

Press **Ctrl+Alt+Shift+Numpad 0** (default) to open the radial menu.

**When to use:**
- Fast production workflows
- When you know what you need
- Keyboard-centric work

**Features:**
- Radial layout for quick selection
- All major functions available
- Closes automatically after selection

## Customize Shortcuts

Go to **Edit > Preferences > Add-ons > LABsystem** to change:
- LABwindow shortcut
- LABmenu shortcut
- Individual module shortcuts
